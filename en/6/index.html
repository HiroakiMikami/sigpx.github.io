The sixth workshop (SIGPX)

SIGPX is a Japanese domestic study group for those interested in programming environments and their programming experience, founded in January 2016. We gather for exchanging information on state-of-the-art research and development projects relevant to programming experience. This page shows the overview of its sixth workshop.

Overview

We are calling for your participation. For this edition of SIGPX, we are anticipating attendees of the relevant Shonan meeting and thus will use English as the official language. Please join us and see the current & future of programming experience!

We are using the Slack team and Google Docs for fostering discussions during and even after the event.

Register to attend☺

  • Location: Walking distance from The University of Tokyo Hongo Campus (Details are disclosed in Slack)
  • Date: 3/2/2019 (Sat) 11:00-16:00
  • Topics: "International situation and information on SIGPX" and "recent research outcomes"
  • Slack channel #sigpx6
